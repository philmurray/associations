Thinkylinks is all about making free associations.

A free association is when you are given a word, then you type the first word that comes to your head.

The object of the game is to make as many free associations as you can before the timer runs out.

Each word is scored based on the free associations of all other Thinkylinks players.

All valid associations will get you 100 points.

You get bonus points if you happen to pick the most commonly associated word.

If you make an association that no player has ever made before, you get just get 1 point.

That's all there is to it! Click on 'Go' to begin!
